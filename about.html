About HITS

HITS is a leading, prestigious, and well-recognized institution in India, located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1985, it offers a wide spectrum of Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Diploma, Research, and Doctoral programs across diverse fields such as Engineering, Technology, Architecture, Management, Law, Fashion Design, Aviation, Applied Sciences, Allied Sciences, and Arts and Sciences. HITS was recognized as a Deemed-to-be University by the UGC in 2008 and received Category-II status from the MHRD, which empowers the institution with academic autonomy. The technical programs at the UG and PG levels are approved by AICTE. The Computer Science and Engineering program is accredited by NBA under the Tier-1 category. HITS is part of the illustrious Hindustan Group of Institutions, which caters to the academic needs of over 15,000 students. The students are encouraged to develop a wide range of hardware- and software-based real-time projects, gaining knowledge and skills in various domains of Computer Applications. They are motivated to be creative, inquisitive, analytical, and detail-oriented. Emphasis is also placed on cultivating teamwork and effective communication skills. In order to promote research, the University provides scholarships to meritorious M.Tech. and Ph.D. scholars. Additionally, AICTE has sanctioned scholarships for GATE-qualified candidates in the M.Tech. programs.
